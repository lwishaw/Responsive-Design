What does Responsive Design solve?

Responsive Design makes it easier for the consumer to use beautiful websites and applications — and for the developer to create these items.
It is better for your business, making the readability on any mobile device accessible. It helps search engines optimize and index your website so that it can be seen by more people.
Almost 60 percent of web traffic is viewed on a mobile device. Responsive Design solves the issue of converting from desktop to mobile — and converting with ease to the multitude of devices on the market that are now available to view your website. Smart watches have entered the mainstream for internet viewing and responsive design can handle it, adapting the layout and content to best meet the users' needs.
Resposive Design helps retain or draw in new customers. If the experience of viewing and interacting with your website is poor, they will have a lower opinion of your company and will head to the competitor's website.
